Privacy Policy

How Flight Control handles account and contact details.

Last updated: May 18, 2026

Information We Collect

When you create an account, Flight Control asks for your name, email address, phone number, and password. When you contact the concierge desk, we may also collect the message you send and the page you sent it from.

How We Use Information

We use account details to let you sign in, remember your profile on your device, and prefill support forms. We use contact details to reply to aviation planning questions, route requests, and support messages.

Password Handling

Passwords are not stored as plain text by the app. The browser creates a salted password hash for local sign-in checks. Your browser may offer to remember the password through its own password manager.

Where Data Is Stored

Account details for this version are stored in your browser on this device. Support messages are sent to the configured Flight Control support email when email delivery is enabled on the server.

Sharing

Flight Control does not sell account details. We share information only when needed to respond to your request, operate email delivery, protect the app, or comply with legal obligations.

Your Choices

You can sign out at any time. You can also clear this site data in your browser to remove locally stored accounts and sessions from the device.
